Jan, 2024

About one year after ChatGPT's release, this blog reflects on its impact on NLP research, examining which new topics have emerged and which older ones have become less relevant. What surprised me most was the speed at which the field has evolved in just a year. I highlighted several "twisted" directions, meaning areas where there has been an unexpected surge or decline in academic interest. In contrast, some "smooth" directions have consistently received significant research attention. By better understanding these shifts in research focus, we can more accurately measure the influence of ChatGPT-related techniques and more effectively prioritize the impactful areas we want to explore next.
